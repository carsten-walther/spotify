Introduction
============

This chapter gives you a basic introduction about the TYPO3 CMS extension “spotify”.

.. _what-it-does:

What does it do?
----------------

A wrapper to let you add spotifys to your site.
This extension will give you the possibility to add spotifys to TYPO3.
